Ramen Eggs

味付け玉子

ajitsuke tamago

These fresh and flavorful eggs are a great addition to ramen and also make for a good snack.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup Mirin
  • 1/4 cup Soy sauce
  • 1 Tbs Brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup sake
  • 6 large eggs

Instructions

  1. Boil water. Remove from heat.
  2. Put in eggs and return to heat. Boil for exactly 6-7 minutes.
  3. While eggs are cooking combine the remaining ingredients and mix well to make the marinade.
  4. Once the eggs are cooked immedietly remove from water and place in ice water.
  5. Once the eggs have cooled, place in the marinade and let them soak up the flavors for at least 12 hours.
